Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 01, 2025 · Last Updated: September 12, 2025

Noble Fir Studio (“Noble Fir,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) values your trust and is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect *

a. Information You Provide to Us

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, business name, and project details when you submit forms (e.g., “Get in Touch,” “Request a Quote,” or “Contact”).
  • Client project information: content, images, and assets you provide for design and development.
  • Billing details: payment-related information for project invoicing and service subscriptions.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device info, and pages visited.
  • Usage data: interactions with our website, such as time on page and navigation paths.
  • Cookies & tracking: used for site functionality, analytics, and performance.

How We Use Your Information *

  • Provide and manage our web design, development, hosting, and SEO services.
  • Respond to inquiries, requests, and support needs.
  • Improve website performance, functionality, and user experience.
  • Send updates, proposals, and project communications.
  • Manage billing, contracts, and service agreements.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce Terms & Conditions.

Sharing of Information *

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information only when:

  • Service providers: trusted vendors (e.g., hosting providers, analytics tools, payment processors) needed to perform our services.
  • Legal obligations: to comply with laws, regulations, or governmental requests.
  • Business transactions: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Data Retention *

We retain information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes in this Policy, provide ongoing services, and meet legal/tax/accounting requirements.

Data Security *

  • Encrypted communication via SSL/TLS on our website.
  • Secure storage and limited access to project files and client information.
  • Regular software and security updates.

Your Rights *

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of your personal data, and request data portability. To exercise your rights, contact us at info@noblefirweb.com.

Third-Party Services *

Our website may link to external services (e.g., social media profiles, analytics, and payment processors). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third parties, and encourage you to review their privacy notices.

Children’s Privacy *

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have such information, please contact us immediately.

Updates to This Policy *

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Updates will be posted here with a revised “Last Updated” date.
